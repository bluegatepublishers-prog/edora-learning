## Positioning

Edora Learning develops secure, intelligent and white-label education technology for publishers, schools, coaching institutes, academies, teachers, students, mentors and parents.

Bluegate Publishers is Edora’s first client but is not the focus of the corporate website. The launch content does not include fabricated statistics, clients, testimonials, awards, logos or team members.

## Visual identity

The public website uses a premium education-technology system led by deep navy, primary and bright blues, sky blue, yellow, gold and orange. Cream, soft blue, soft yellow and soft orange establish alternating section rhythm. Manrope is the single type family, with expressive heavy headings and restrained gradient emphasis.

Buttons use three shared treatments: orange-to-gold primary, blue-outline secondary and deep-navy dark. Cards use varied accent borders, icon panels, background colours and layered shadows rather than one repeated treatment.

## CTA link audit

- Global Request a Demo links use `/contact?interest=demo` and preselect Edora Education OS.
- Publisher, school, coaching and academy CTAs use their matching contact query.
- AI CTAs use `/contact?interest=ai`.
- Partnership CTAs use `/contact?interest=partnership` and may prefill a partnership subject.
- Audience cards use existing solution anchors: `#publishers`, `#schools`, `#coaching`, `#academies`, `#educators` and `#learners`.
- No CTA uses an empty link, `#`, a missing page or an unfinished route.

## Public product boundaries

- Communication Hub is labelled “Coming Next.”

- AI is not described as infallible and no model, provider, prompt or quota internals are exposed.
